About Us
Hello! We are Gift of Music, founded by Sophia and Claire Buda. Based in Glendale, California, we are all highschool students who are passionate about music, and we want to give back to our community by sharing our music with others. As of now, we are focused on playing music at nursing homes in the LA area, but we plan to expand to other venues such as shelters and hospitals.
